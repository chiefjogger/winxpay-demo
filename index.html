01At the till

Walk into a tạp hóa. Open the app.
Pay in five seconds.

On the left, the WinX Tạp Hóa POS — a Zalo Mini-App that runs on Cô Lan's phone. On the right, the WinXPay consumer wallet on the shopper's phone. The two talk to each other over a single VietQR. The Pay-in-4 leg is underwritten by Techcombank in the background.

1
Scan
Cashier rings up the basket. POS shows a single QR — "Pay now" or "Pay in 4".
2
Underwrite
If the shopper picks Pay-in-4, WinXPay asks Techcombank to approve. Decision arrives in seconds.
~1.6 s
3
Settle
Merchant gets paid instantly into their wallet. Same as a normal QR transfer — no waiting, no T+2.
4
Auto-debit
Shopper pays back over four weekly installments via auto-debit from their TCB account. Zero interest.
5
Receipt
E-invoice issued automatically (NĐ 70/2025 compliant). Receipt lands in the wallet, in chat, on paper — wherever you want.
A
Runs on a phone
No new POS terminal. The shopkeeper opens our Zalo Mini-App and rings up the basket. Same phone they already have.
B
Discount at the till
When the shopper pays with WinXPay, eligible items get a discount funded by the brand, not the shop. The cashier just sees a lower total.
C
A real VietQR
The QR uses NAPAS 247 — the same national rail every Vietnamese bank speaks. Settlement to the merchant's wallet is instant.

03For merchants

One line of code.
QR + Pay-in-4, dropped into any checkout.

Merchants on the Zalo Mini-App POS get WinXPay automatically — it's just another payment button. For everyone else, integration is a single embed: a button on the page, an API call from the server, or a webhook. No bespoke contracts. No new POS hardware.

⚡
Drop-in checkout button
One HTML attribute, or one SDK call from your server. Renders the WinXPay QR plus the Pay-in-4 option in any web page, app, or POS surface.
🔁
Webhook-driven settlement
Real-time payment.captured and bnpl.approved events fire as soon as the shopper confirms. Money lands in the merchant wallet instantly.
🧾
E-invoice baked in (NĐ 70/2025)
A compliant HĐĐT-MTT is issued for every settled transaction. The shopkeeper never touches a tax form. Customer can email it, print it, or scan a QR receipt.
🏦
No credit risk for the merchant
Pay-in-4 is underwritten by Techcombank — not by the shop. The merchant always sees the full amount, settled today, regardless of how the shopper chose to pay.
🪪
No new hardware
Works on a phone, on a counter tablet, or printed as a static QR. If the merchant can show a QR, they can accept WinXPay.
checkout.html · drop-in widget
<!-- Add the WinXPay button to any HTML page --> <script src="https://js.winxpay.vn/v1/sdk.js"></script> <button data-winxpay="checkout" data-amount="200000" data-currency="VND" data-merchant="MS001247" data-bnpl="true" data-discount-id="WXP-DISC-Q2"> Trả 200K hoặc 4 × 50K </button>
server.ts · capture + webhook
import { WinXPay } from '@winxpay/sdk'; const wxp = new WinXPay({ apiKey: process.env.WXP_KEY }); // Create a checkout — receive QR + paymentId const checkout = await wxp.checkouts.create({ amount: 200_000, basket: cart.items, bnpl: 'auto', // auto-decide via TCB brandFundedDiscount: true, // pulls eligible brand budget callbackUrl: 'https://store.vn/wxp' }); // Webhook handler — fires when consumer scans + TCB approves app.post('/wxp', (req) => { if (req.body.event === 'payment.captured') { orders.markPaid(req.body.merchantRef); // money lands in the merchant wallet, instant } });
04How it works

Four surfaces.
One quiet rail underneath.

WinXPay is a single product that shows up in four places. Whichever surface you start from — your wallet, the shopkeeper's till, a chat with your favourite store, or a brand promo — the underlying QR, settlement, and Pay-in-4 logic is the same.

SURFACE 01
Consumer wallet
A standalone iOS & Android app, plus a Mini-App inside Zalo. Top-up, scan, pay, split, get receipts. Pay-in-4 is a button, not a separate product.
Quét trả · Pay in 4 · Lịch sử · Quà
SURFACE 02
Merchant POS
A Zalo Mini-App for tạp hóa shopkeepers — runs on the phone they already own. Ring up the basket, show the QR, see the money land. No terminal to buy.
Giỏ hàng · Quét · Hoá đơn điện tử
SURFACE 03
In-chat checkout
An AI agent that lives inside any Zalo Official Account. Suggests baskets, takes orders, opens WinXPay to settle. Receipt comes back to the same conversation.
Recommend · Checkout · E-invoice
SURFACE 04
Drop-in for any merchant
A button, an SDK, a webhook. Lets any web shop, app, or POS accept WinXPay with the same QR + Pay-in-4 selector. No bespoke contract, no hardware.
data-winxpay · /sdk · /webhook
Underneath, always

⚡
